# Confirmations

Confirm at action time before:
- Sending messages, posting comments, or submitting forms that create external side effects.
- Making purchases, booking travel, changing account permissions, or changing sharing settings.
- Uploading personal files or downloading files into sensitive locations.
- Deleting nontrivial data.
- Installing or enabling browser extensions, apps, or software.
- Saving passwords, payment methods, addresses, or other sensitive account data.
- Accepting browser permission prompts for camera, microphone, location, downloads, extension installation, or account access.

The confirmation should name the destination site or account, the data involved, and the exact action.
